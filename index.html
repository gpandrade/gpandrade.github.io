About Me

I'm a first year Ph.D. student in computer science at CU Boulder. My research interests are in complex adaptive systems, unconventional models of computing, and collective intelligence. I'm a part of the CS Theory group and my advisor is Professor Joshua Grochow. Previously, I worked with Professor Robert Kozma as part of the Biologically Inspired Neural and Dynamical Systems Laboratory at UMass Amherst while pursueing my M.S. in applied math. During that time, I also worked as a graduate IT assistant in the department of mathematics & statistics.
